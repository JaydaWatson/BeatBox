About Us

Beat Box is a custom subscription box filled with self care products, each month customers will recieve a box of their choice. There are three options to choose from Platinum, Gold, and Premium. The Platinum plan is the highest plan which offers everything Beat Box has to offer while, the Gold Plan offers similar products but not everything that we offer and lastly Premium is more of a trail run to see if Beat Box is the perfect fit for you. Each month the boxes will be filled with a variety of self care products as well as different scents and flavors. As well as each customer will be able to obtain a custom Beat Box bonnet. Beat Box is targeted to those who use products as such; Lashes, Compact Mirrors, Lip Gloss, Candles, Eye masks, and Bonnets. Beat Box is constantly growing and continues to recieve feedback from customers to provide the perfect box for each customer. If one wishes to purchase and item individually they will be available on our shop page.
